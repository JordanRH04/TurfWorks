About Us

14 years of the same crew, showing up on time

Turf Works started as a single mower and a handful of neighborhood accounts. It's grown, but the promise hasn't: one crew, one call, no surprises.

Our Story

Built on repeat customers, not ad spend

Turf Works was founded in Atlanta in 2012 on a simple idea: homeowners don't want to manage a service provider, they want a yard that's handled. No call centers, no rotating crews, no surprise invoices.

Fourteen years later, most of our new clients still come from a neighbor's referral. We keep it that way by staying small enough to know every property on the route by name.
